# Dashboard Brief - Employee Attrition

## Audience and purpose

This dashboard is for an HR business partner or people analytics reviewer who needs to understand where attrition risk and replacement-cost exposure are concentrated.

## Pages

### 1 - Executive KPI

Headline KPIs: headcount, leavers, attrition rate, estimated replacement cost, and average cost per leaver. Visuals: attrition by department and overtime split.

### 2 - Attrition Diagnostics

Break down attrition by job role, tenure band, income quartile, satisfaction, and overtime. The key question is not "what perfectly predicts attrition" but which segments deserve a retention conversation.

### 3 - Retention Decision Support

Show the flight-risk segment table and cost exposure by department. Use this page to discuss which retention plays are worth testing and which assumptions drive cost estimates.

## Honesty notes

The source data is synthetic. Cost values are planning estimates from documented assumptions, not observed finance records.
